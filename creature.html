About Rabbits

Rabbits are small mammals with fluffy, short tails,whiskers and distinctive long ears.
There are more than 30 species around the world,
and while they live in many different environments,
they have many things in common. Rabbits and
hares are in the same taxanomic family, Leporidae,
but they are in different genera.
